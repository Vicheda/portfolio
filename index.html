    • JCI Cambodia

    In Cambodia, menstrual talks are not to be spoken openly, “it’s embarrassing” they say. Girls were never properly taught the subject and only learned how to deal with periods from our mothers, sisters, or close female relatives. This problem can cause severe health problems, physically and emotionally. Hence, it has to be addressed.

    We provide menstrual education by hosting workshops and book distributions at schools.
